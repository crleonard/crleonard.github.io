CrazyCogs

Can you solve the puzzle?

The aim is simple, match the dropping shapes into their corresponding holes in the cogs below.
Sound easy.. Well it's not as easy as it sounds, each time a shape is successfully matched into the correct hole, the next release will speed up. The longer you manage to match, the faster the shapes will release.

This is a great way to both have fun, and to relax. With the integration of Game Center, each time you set a new high score it will be uploaded to the leaderboard. This is a great way to keep you entertained, by both beating your friends and also the other players. The leaderboard is in 2 parts, overall and your friends, so you can check at any time.
